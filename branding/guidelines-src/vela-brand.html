01 · The brand

A sail, not a robot

Vela means sail. The brand promises effortless forward motion: a seller posts on Instagram, and the shop takes shape by itself — products, payments, orders.

Idea

Instagram brings the customers; Vela does everything Instagram doesn't — checkout, card payments in Lek, inventory, orders. The wind is already there; Vela is the sail that catches it.

Tagline

Albanian (primary)English
Kthe Instagramin në dyqan onlineTurn your Instagram into an online store

Brand principles

PrincipleWhat it means in practice
EffortlessEverything happens "vetë" (by itself). Copy leads with the outcome, not the technology. The word "AI" is never used — say sistemi / the system.
Warm energyThe palette is warm magenta with red and amber — commerce, sun, momentum — never corporate blue.
Instagram-nativeThe brand visibly descends from the Instagram gradient. Sellers should feel Vela is the natural next step for their profile.
Albanian-firstAlbanian is the default language everywhere; English is the secondary locale. Prices in Lek (ALL).
Alive, never noisyGradients drift, counters count, cards lift — always subtle, and always disabled for reduced-motion users.

A paper-boat ship drawn in the heritage Instagram gradient. It sails right — forward, to the customer.

Rules

  • Clear space: keep at least the height of the ship's hull free on all sides.
  • Minimum size: lockup 24 px tall on screen / 8 mm in print; icon alone 16 px / 5 mm.
  • Colour: use the full-colour gradient mark on light backgrounds; the white mark on dark or gradient backgrounds. Monochrome black only where colour is unavailable.
  • Don't recolour, rotate, outline, add shadows, stretch, or set the wordmark in another typeface.
  • Don't place the full-colour mark on the brand gradient — use the white version.

CSS utility .brand-gradient — also available clipped to text (.brand-text) and as an animated border (.grad-border: 120° pan through #D946EF → #EF4444 → #F59E0B → #DB2777).

Where it may appear

  • Hero headlines (gradient text), the primary CTA (BrandButton), splash screen, scroll-progress bar, featured pricing border, upgrade prompts.
  • Don't use it for body text, icons, large background areas behind reading content, or more than one hero element per screen.

Heritage gradient (Instagram)

The classic Instagram spectrum survives in the logo mark, the splash screen, story-film props and confetti — it links Vela to where its sellers come from. Never use it for UI controls.

06 · Shape, surface & motion

Soft, elevated, alive

Corner radius

lg · 24 px (cards, modals)
md · 16 px (buttons, inputs)
sm · 8 px (chips, badges)

Base token --radius: 1.5rem. Generous rounding is a brand signature — when unsure, round more.

Elevation

Shadows are soft, layered and faintly violet-tinted (base rgba(46, 33, 64, …)) so elevation reads as glass, not grey drop-shadow. An optional glassmorphism mode renders surfaces at card/70 + 20px backdrop blur.

Motion

ElementBehaviour
Brand gradientbrand-blob: 13 s ease-in-out, infinite alternate — four blobs drift on independent paths; movement feels organic, never looping.
Gradient bordersbrand-pan: 9 s ease-in-out background pan.
Entrances / scrollGSAP: short y-offsets (20–30 px), 0.7–0.8 s, power3.out, small staggers.
HoverCards lift via shadow + ≤2 px translate, 0.22–0.35 s ease.
AccessibilityEvery animation is disabled under prefers-reduced-motion — no exceptions.

07 · Voice & language

Direct, warm, jargon-free

Vela speaks like a helpful peer, not a tech vendor. Albanian first, English second. Short sentences. Outcomes, not features.

Vocabulary — enforced app-wide

Never sayAlbanianEnglish
AI / Artificial Intelligencesistemithe system
DM / DMsmesazhemessages
Feedpostimetposts
eCommerce platform / solutiondyqan onlineonline shop

Tone examples

Example
Do"Sistemi i kthen postimet e tua në dyqan të vërtetë. Pa kod."
Do"Klientët blejnë vetë — pa mesazhe."
Do"Provo falas 7 ditë · pa kartë · anulo kurdo"
Don't"AI-powered eCommerce solution leveraging machine learning."
Don'tExclamation marks in error states, or blame: errors say what happened and what to do next.

Mechanics

  • Currency: always Lek — formatted 3.500 L in UI, ALL in formal/pricing contexts.
  • Both locales ship together; never leave a string untranslated.
  • Emoji are allowed in marketing mock-ups and celebratory moments (🎉 on order success), never in system or error copy.
  • Speak to one person ("ti" form in Albanian): "dyqani yt", "postimet e tua".
